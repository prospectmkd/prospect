About us

How we built a bridge between global ambition and elite remote talent.

Our Founding Story: A New Vision for Remote Talent

At Prospect, we believe remote hiring shouldn’t be complicated. We were founded by professionals who saw first‑hand how businesses struggled with mismatched talent, cultural gaps, and communication barriers when outsourcing. From there, we set out to do things differently—focusing on quality, transparency, and long-term partnership.

We launched our operations in Skopje, North Macedonia, but our vision has always been global—to equip companies everywhere with access to the top 3% of remote professionals, and to offer world-class opportunities to talent from regions that are often overlooked.

Why Prospect & Why Eastern Europe?

Eastern Europe, and North Macedonia in particular, is a hidden gem in the world of remote talent. We understand both Western business standards and regional cultures. This unique positioning allows us to act as a trusted bridge—providing talent that is not only technically excellent, but also culturally fluent, reliable, and cost-effective.
