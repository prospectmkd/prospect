Our Recruitment Process

Structured, transparent, and designed to connect you with the top 2% of remote talent worldwide.

Step-by-Step Hiring

From discovery to onboarding, we make remote recruitment simple and effective. Our method ensures quality, efficiency, and long-term fit.

Step-by-Step Hiring

From discovery to onboarding, we make remote recruitment simple and effective. Our method ensures quality, efficiency, and long-term fit.

1. Discovery Call

We start by understanding your business goals, hiring needs, and company culture. This ensures we target candidates who are not only skilled but also aligned with your values.

Duration: 30-45 min | Key Outcome: Clear job specification
2. Talent Sourcing

We tap into a global network of top talent, ensuring candidates meet technical, cultural, and role-specific criteria before presenting them to you.

Duration: 5-7 days | Key Outcome: Curated candidate shortlist
3. Screening & Assessment

Candidates undergo technical tests, interviews, and cultural assessments. Only pre-qualified candidates reach your desk.

Duration: 1 week | Key Outcome: Top candidates ready for interview
4. Client Interviews

We schedule interviews, provide guidance, and help collect feedback. You meet only the candidates who match your expectations.

Duration: 3-5 days | Key Outcome: Selected hire
5. Onboarding

We manage contracts, payroll, and introductions to ensure a smooth transition for new hires into your team.

Duration: 1-2 days | Key Outcome: Fully onboarded employee
6. Ongoing Support

We provide ongoing HR and payroll support, regular check-ins, and assistance to ensure long-term success.

Duration: Continuous | Key Outcome: High retention & satisfaction

Why Choose Prospect?

Partnering with us brings multiple advantages for your remote hiring journey:

Top Talent

Access the top 2% of global professionals, fully vetted and ready to work remotely.

Time-Saving

We handle sourcing, screening, and onboarding, reducing your recruitment time drastically.

Reliable Support

Ongoing HR, payroll, and operational support ensures your remote team thrives long-term.
